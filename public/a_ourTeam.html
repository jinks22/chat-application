About Us

Teledata employs dynamic individuals with a passion and depth in their respective areas of expertise.

Teledata’s team is full of knowledgeable, driven, and results-oriented individuals who are aimed at building meaningful strategic relationships. We believe in being connected to our customers in a manner that allows us to understand their business thoroughly.
This provides us with insight and commitment in order to provide the right services and solutions.

At Teledata, we define great customer service as

Doing the right thing.
Owning our customers’ issues from start to finish.
Exceeding the expectations.
Being courteous and professional.
Building lasting relationships.

Teledata aims to be a company where Volunteerism and Care for others is greatly valued.
We hope to establish an organization wherein employees are geared towards creating a better world not just today but for the future generation.
Over the years, Teledata has been partnering with various Government and Non-government organization in hopes to provide help to the less fortunate members of our community.
Through these efforts, we wish to be a part of a country where there is hope for a better tomorrow.
